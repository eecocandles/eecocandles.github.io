Our Story

Ember & Oak began as a kitchen-table passion — small-batches poured with attention and care. We source sustainable waxes and clean fragrances, hand-pour every jar, and test every scent to ensure a long, even burn. Each candle is crafted to bring warmth and gentle calm to your home.

We believe in quality over quantity: intentional scents, beautiful packaging, and a mindful approach to materials.
